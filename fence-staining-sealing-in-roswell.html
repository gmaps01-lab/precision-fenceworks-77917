Frequently Asked Questions About Fence Staining & Sealing

How much does fence staining and sealing typically cost in the Roswell, GA area?
Pricing for fence staining and sealing in Roswell, GA depends on several factors, including the type of wood, fence height, total linear feet, and the condition of the fence. For most residential projects, you can expect costs to range from $2.50 to $5.00 per linear foot. We provide free, no-obligation estimates after an onsite assessment to give you an accurate quote tailored to your fence’s size and needs.
How long does the staining and sealing process take, and will weather in Roswell affect the timeline?
A typical backyard fence takes 1-2 days to stain and seal, depending on its size and condition. Weather in Roswell, especially during the humid summers or rainy seasons, can impact scheduling. We monitor forecasts closely and only apply stain and sealer when conditions are dry and temperatures are optimal (ideally between 50°F and 90°F). If we anticipate weather delays, we’ll communicate with you promptly to reschedule and ensure the best possible finish.
Is Precision Fenceworks licensed and insured to perform fence staining and sealing in Georgia?
Yes, Precision Fenceworks is fully licensed and insured to provide fence staining and sealing services in Roswell and throughout Georgia. Our team is trained in proper application techniques and safety protocols, ensuring your property and our crew are protected throughout the project.
What is the process for staining and sealing a fence, and do I need to prepare anything beforehand?
Our process starts with a thorough inspection and cleaning of your fence. We remove any dirt, mildew, or old coatings as needed—often using a gentle power wash. Once the fence is dry, we apply a high-quality stain and sealer with professional equipment to ensure even coverage. We recommend moving any items or plants away from the fence before our arrival. If you have pets, please keep them indoors during the work for their safety and to allow the stain to dry properly.
